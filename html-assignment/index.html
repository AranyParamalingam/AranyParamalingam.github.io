About Me

My name is Arany Paramalingam and I am from Toronto. My interests include music and visual arts. Some of the music I listen to are J-rock, K-rap, British rap, Polish EDM, and Soca. I tend to listen to music I don't understand, because I feel like the lyrics get in the way of my enjoyment of the music. I love art and looking at many art pieces from varies artists online. My favorite art movements are Surrealism and Realism.

Past my interests, I am currently attending the Interactive Media and Design course at Algonquin College. I decided to take this course due to its variety. I honestly didn't know what career I wanted to pursue in at the beginning of grade 12, I new the general area but not the job title. So far I believe I want to be a UX designer or a web developer, but I feel like I can truly know what I really want to be when I'm preparing to go into coop. Overall, I hope I enjoy my time here in this class and course!
