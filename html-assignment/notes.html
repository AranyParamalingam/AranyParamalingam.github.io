HTML Notes

Some HTML Tags I have Learnt so far

Emphasis
The emphasis tag, emphasizes slightly more important text
Headings
The headings tag, makes a certain piece of text, a heading. This tag has a variation of sizes from 1 to 6.
Important text
The important text tag, emphasizes important text by bolding the text.
The Image Element
The image element tag, displays an image from your folder to your website.
HTML Anchors
The ancor tag, creates a link on our website and can be used to link to other pages of our site.
